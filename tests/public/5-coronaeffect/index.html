Topics data resources

Pixonal is not responsible of the data presented in the visualization

  • Covid-19

    The Covid-19 data is populated through API from github.com/pomber/covid19

  • Number of flights

    Daily flights data is exported and updated weekly from flightradar24.com

  • Bitcoin

    The BTC daily price is exported and updated weekly from Yahoo Finance

  • Zoom Stock Price

    ZM stock price is exported and updated weekly from Yahoo Finance

  • Nasdaq

    Nasdaq Index is exported and updated weekly from Yahoo Finance

  • S&P 500

    S&P 500 Index is exported and updated weekly from Yahoo Finance

  • Oil Price

    Crude Oil daily price is exported and updated weekly from Yahoo Finance

  • Box Office

    Daily Box Office domestic sales in the US is exported and updated weekly from boxofficemojo.com

  • Entertainment Revenue

    Entertainment revenue data is collected and aggregated by Debt Agency

  • FMCG Revenue

    FMCG Revenue data is collected and aggregated by Debt Agency

  • Travel Transactions

    Travel Transactions data is collected and aggregated by Debt Agency

  • Government Measures

    Global government measures is collected and exported from ACAPS

  • Number of Lockdowns

    National Lockdowns is collected and exported from ACAPS

  • Restaurant Bookings

    The change in restaurant bookings is collected from Open Table

  • School National Closures

    Number of school closures locally and nationally is exported from UNESCO

Coronaffect visualization is a contribution from Pixonal to help the world make more sense of the Covid-19 data through data visualization and interaction. The tool helps visualize the pattern fo Covid-19 with general topics and stories; it also helps in uploading any data-set to be visualized on top of the Covid-19 graph.

Pixonal is an award-winning data design and consulting agency based out of Dubai specialized in converting data into interactive stories, helping governments and organizations take informed decisions through design.
